Controls:
Hold R: ride the V A P O R W A V E C O W
F: Move camera to right side in front of car
G: Move camera to Left side behind car
H: Reset position W/S: Move Forward / Backward
A/D : Move Left / Right
Space/C : Move Up / Down
Q/E : Roll (Tilt Left / Right)
Left/Right arrow: Yaw (Rotate Left / Right)
Up/Down arrow: Pitch (Rotate Up / Down)
Mousewheel scroll: zoom in / out
Can also click and drag with mouse to rotate camera
